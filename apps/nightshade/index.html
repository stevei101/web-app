Paradigm Shift: AI-Assisted vs. Agent-Driven

The Baseline

Current AI coding tools (Copilots) rely on human initiation and single-file context. They are assistants, not participants.

  • ⚠️ Manual trigger required for every action.
  • ⚠️ Limited to local IDE context.
  • ⚠️ High cognitive load on the developer.

The Evolution

"Agentic SDLC" injects autonomous, event-driven AI agents directly into the CI/CD pipeline. Self-triggering AI workflows.

  • 🚀 Velocity: Drastically reduce PR lead times and MTTR.
  • 🛡️ Consistency: Enforce enterprise-grade security & governance natively.
  • ⚖️ Flexibility: Vendor-neutral (Google Vertex AI & IBM watsonx).

🗺️ Execution Strategy & Roadmap

Rapid adaptation via Agile delivery. This interactive timeline details the sequence of Epics required to move from infrastructure provisioning to a high-impact organizational pilot in just 6 weeks.

Weeks 1-2

Epic 1: Foundation

Provision the GCP backbone (Cloud Run, Cloud Build) and the MongoDB Atlas cluster via Terraform/Crossplane. Establish baseline DORA metrics.

Weeks 3-4

Epic 2: Orchestration

Deploy the TypeScript/Bun API Gateway. Implement the vendor-neutral routing abstraction layer mapping to Vertex AI and watsonx endpoints.

Epic 3: Worker Dev

Build out the Rust AST parsers and package the high-speed local developer CLI tooling. Establish webhook connections to GitLab/GitHub.

Week 6+

Epic 4: Pilot & Scale

Launch the automated PR review agent on 2–3 target repositories. Prove ROI, measure pipeline acceleration, and present the executive success report.
